Giving is Virtue.

Since our inception, we have worked tirelessly to uplift the lives of our fellow citizens, thanks to the unwavering support of our community. Notably, esteemed partners like Dahabshiil and the esteemed Mayor of Hargeisa, Mr. Abdikarim Mooge, have joined hands with us, exemplifying the spirit of collaboration and shared purpose.

Giving Back

"At Abu huraira Organization Charity, we believe in the profound impact of giving back. Our commitment to the community is a promise to return the kindness we've received tenfold. Through our dedicated efforts and your support, we strive to give back the hope, love, and opportunities that empower individuals and transform communities."

Working together

"Collaboration is the cornerstone of progress, and at Abu huraira Organization Charity, we understand the power of working together. We unite individuals, organizations, and communities to harness their collective strength. Together, we build bridges, break barriers, and create a brighter, more inclusive future for all."

Helping hand

"A helping hand can change lives. At Abu huraira Organization Charity, extending that hand of compassion is at the core of our mission. We reach out to those in need, offering not just assistance but the promise of a better tomorrow. Your support ensures that no one faces adversity alone, for together."
